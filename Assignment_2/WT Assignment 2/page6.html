Stay True

Hua Hsu

★ ★ ★ ★ ★ 5.00 9,344 rating , 950 reviews

Stay True is a 2022 memoir by Hua Hsu, published by Anchor Books and
Doubleday, both imprints of Penguin Random House. The memoir depicts
Hsu's unlikely relationship with a college friend named Ken, a friendship
that was unexpectedly and tragically cut short when Ken was killed in
a carjacking in 1998.
The book received the 2023 Pulitzer Prize for Memoir or Autobiography
as well as the 2022 National Book Critics Circle award for memoir and autobiography.
